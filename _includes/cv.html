About Me

I'm a Project Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology at the University of Tokyo, working with Prof. Yinqiang Zheng. Before joining UTokyo, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Advanced Interactive Technologies lab and AI Center at ETH Zürich.

I received a B.S. degree and a Ph.D. degree in software engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2018 and 2023, respectively. My advisor was Prof. Feng Xu.

My research interests include dynamic reconstruction, machine learning, and 3D animation. I have extensive experience in multi-view capture systems. During my time at ETH Zürich, I contributed to the Volumetric Vision Hub, a large-scale multi-camera capture facility, and led the capture system development for Vestir, an AI-powered virtual fitting room startup.
